Frequently Asked Questions

How many revisions are included?
▼
Each package includes up to two rounds of revisions. This gives you the opportunity to request changes and fine-tune designs to match your vision. Additional revision rounds can be added as a premium add-on for $20 per round.
What file formats will I receive?
▼
By default, all designs are delivered as Canva files for easy editing. PNG with high-resolution versions and PDF formats are included for immediate use.
Do you work with international clients?
▼
Absolutely! I work with clients from around the world. Time zone differences are not an issue—communication is handled through email, WhatsApp, and Canva collaboration tools. I'm happy to accommodate different schedules and currencies for all international projects.
How long does a project take?
▼
Standard turnaround time is 5-7 business days. Rush delivery (24 hours) and same-day delivery options are available as premium add-ons if you need faster results. Exact timelines depend on project complexity and current workload.
